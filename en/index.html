Maple Bear Caxias do Sul is the only Canadian bilingual school in Caxias do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul — welcoming children from 18 months to elementary school with daily English immersion. If your family is moving to the Serra Gaúcha region, this page is for you.

About the school

Part of a global network, rooted in your new city.

We are part of the Maple Bear global network — more than 600 schools in over 30 countries, delivering Canadian education adapted to local curricula. In Caxias do Sul, that means a school where children genuinely live in two languages every day, not one where English is just another subject on the timetable.

Our teachers follow the Canadian methodology: learning through inquiry, play, and projects, with literacy developed in English and Portuguese side by side. Classrooms are English-immersion environments in the early years, and the academic program is fully aligned with the Brazilian national curriculum (BNCC) — so your child meets every Brazilian requirement while learning the way Canadian students do.

Relocating to Caxias do Sul?

A smooth landing for your child.

Caxias do Sul is the industrial hub of the Serra Gaúcha — home to global manufacturers, a strong wine and food culture, and a growing community of international families. Moving here with children raises one big question: where will they feel at home at school?

Families who relocate choose Maple Bear because the transition is gentle in both directions. A child arriving from abroad keeps learning in English from day one while picking up Portuguese naturally among Brazilian classmates. A child who may move again later carries international curriculum continuity — the Maple Bear network spans more than 30 countries, and the Canadian approach travels well to schools worldwide.

Just as important: your child won't be isolated in an "international bubble". Our students are part of the local community, fluent in the language and culture of their new city, while keeping English as a true second mother tongue.
